Yummy homemade salsa recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 jalapeno chile peppers
  • 5 cloves garlic,finely chopped
  • 1 onion,finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 whole bundle of cilantro
  • 1 can of diced green chile peppers
  • 1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • 2 (14 oz) can whole peeled fire roasted tomatoes

Recipe Steps

  1. Prepare jaleno chile peppers, garlic, and onions
  2. Blend together 1 can of whole peeled fire roasted tomatoes with jalapeno chile peppers, garlic, onion, salt, and can of green chile peppers
  3. Add in the 1 can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers and other can of whole peeled fire roasted tomatoes
  4. Blend until salsa has reached desired consistency
  5. Transfer salsa into medium bowl
  6. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving
  7. Enjoy!
